# Experience Antigua, Guatemala

Come visit Antigua, Guatemala with [ Hotel Terra Maya](https://www.hotelterramaya.com/) and [3 Hermanos Antigua](https://3hermanosantigua.com/) and experience genuine Guatemalan life and culture.

## What we offer

- **Accommodations** — Comfortable stays at our hotel and guesthouses
- **Transport** — Airport pickups, shuttles between destinations, and day-trip logistics
- **Tours** — Guided volcano hikes, coffee farm visits, market tours, and more
- **Local cuisine** — Authentic Guatemalan meals and restaurant recommendations

## Who is this for?

Whether you have a group of students, a youth group, a church group, or even just a few friends — we can put together a great experience you won't forget.

If you'd like to turn it into a mission trip or do some volunteering, we can orchestrate opportunities to work alongside local schools or religious organizations.
